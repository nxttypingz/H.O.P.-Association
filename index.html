The Life Of Someone Else
Is Just As Valuable As Yours

From ofhsoupkitchen.org

[Mission]

Many lives suffer for the unimaginable almost everyday. Our mission is to give comfort to those who suffer greatly through anyway we can; physically, financially, e.t.c.

[Why/What?]

In today's world, many unpredicitable events occur, most times it is a great friend or companion we lost or we miss a special thing. For some, things like these seem worthless but there are others who suffer a greater cause. We saw them at least once in our lives or heard a past person's story but it is sickening to see no change occur. Most of these unfortunate events are though poverty, homelessness, death or some sort of lose.

[Why are people selfish to those in need?]

Nowadays, when it comes to helping one in need or doing a favor, we are quick to avoid the chance and give a poor excuse of not helping them out. Most believe there is a good reason for everything but that is not always the case. It takes only a few seconds to a few minutes to give or even donate to a cause, but we, 'never find the time' to.
