[Who are those who suffer?]

Most times when someone mentions a person who is suffering, we automatically think of someone who is homeless or has just lost a job or loved one. Though those are some examples of one who has suffered from an unfortunate situation, going through an unfortunate situation includes many more factors, especially the aftermath. The results of this are most times, a mental issue, a setback with time or plans and or any other health issues.


There is a YouTube channel out there named, 'AnthonyPadilla' who mostly covers interviews of different types of videos and by luck he has done two, made just for your viewing. They are quite legnthy but are worth the watch.


These two videos depict those who suffered from a situation but survived.
We can do this the same way, not in the same manner shown in the video but to help those who suffer to refrain from these situations. It takes a lot of guts to put such an event live on the internet but the amount of lives you inspire and help will save that lost.

Futher Information


This organization and website was made for the purpose to help those that have been or are in a difficult/unfortunate situation. By sharing platforms, stories and other resources to look for, we hope to bring more into aiding such people and let their voices finally be heard.
We would also like to make this page group based. Any and all information is creditted to the respectful owners. If you have a story, share it with us!
